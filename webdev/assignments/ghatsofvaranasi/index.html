The Ghats Of Varanasi

Situated On The Banks Of The Holy Ganga, the "city of light" is a major centre of spirituality and self realisation. Termed as Rudravasa in vedic texts, the city is considered to be the city of Lord Shiva himself, his presence can be felt resounding in the mighty Kashi Vishwanath Temple. The city is a destination for self seekers and pursuers of god consiousness.

From Assi Ghat to the Kashi Vishwanath temple being the beacons of spirituality || to the Banaras Hindu University and Banaras Sanskrit College being ace institutions of knowledge || From Sarnath where Gautam Buddha gave his first words of teaching and where the Ashoka Pillar stands tall || to sites where epics in the Avadhi language like the Ramcharitmanas and dohas of poets like Kabir Das and Ravi Das were penned ||

Here we present a list of famous heritage sites and tourist spots in the city of Varanasi along with a brief description of the experience that one can get by visiting the sites.
