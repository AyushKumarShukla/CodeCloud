The Kashi Vishwanath Temple

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is situated near the banks of the river Ganga in Varanasi | Uttar Pradesh. The temple has remained a major center for pilgrims and teachers of spirituality. Major leading Indian personalities like Adi Shankaracharya | Ramkrishna Paramhansa | Swami Vivekananda | Goswami Tulsidas | etc have been associated with the temple.

The temple receives around three thousands visitors everyday, the number jumps to above one lakh and more on special occasions and festivals.

The temple is also an architectural marvel, it contains a 15.5 metre high golden dome and similar smaller domes each of which are made up of pure gold. All these experiences make the temple a must visit site for someone touring Varanasi or a nearby place.
